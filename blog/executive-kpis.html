5 KPIs Every Executive Dashboard Needs

Jun 2025 • 3 min read

Start with decisions, then metrics

Great dashboards drive decisions. Begin with the decisions leaders make weekly or monthly, then design KPIs that inform those calls.

Five KPIs that rarely fail

  1. Revenue Quality — growth adjusted for churn/discounts.
  2. Unit Economics — margin per unit/customer/cohort.
  3. Pipeline Health — volume, velocity, win rate.
  4. Operational Throughput — cycle time and bottlenecks.
  5. Cash Conversion — AR/AP/Inventory dynamics.

Design the semantic model carefully, document definitions, and use row‑level security for trust.
